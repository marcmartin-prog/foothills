How We Started

Foothills Overhead Doors LLC was established with a commitment to quality, customer service, value, and, most of all, making your Complete Satisfaction our Goal. Due to the success of this simple business goal, we have become what we are today - the Northeast Arkansas overhead door sales and service company of quality and value in the full line of products we offer. From standard sectional panel overhead doors in residential and commercial styles, to high-speed doors, counter shutters, fire doors, security grills, and so much more, we have the options you are looking for.

We are an installer/employee-owned family-managed business based in Strawberry, AR giving us the ability to efficiently service all of Northeast to North Central Arkansas. Our in-house-trained, part-owner installers and service technicians are dedicated to doing their best for you in all they do. We carry the full product lines of CHI Overhead Doors Inc., Liftmaster Openers and Operators, Rytec High Speed Doors, Janus International Inc., Advanced Door Technologies Inc., and other specialty doors so as to have the door you need for your specific application. We especially want to extend a thank you to CHI Overhead Doors Inc. and their staff for all their assistance to us and our customers. Without them we would not be where we are today.

We are not here just to sell you a product, we are here to install it and service it in a way that you can be proud of. Give us a call today and join our family, for the good of your family or business.
